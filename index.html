Glossary

What do these terms mean?

Points

Earned by recycling items. Points accumulate in your class pool and convert to rewards at a threshold.

Class Pool

The total reward currency your class has earned. This is split evenly among all students.

Lifetime

The total number of items your class has ever recycled.

Threshold

The point target that triggers automatic conversion to reward currency.

Streak

Consecutive days your class has submitted items.

Simple & Rewarding

How It Works

1. Collect & Log

Bring recyclable items to class and log them through your class portal using your 6-digit code.

2. Earn Points

Every item earns points for your class pool. Hit the threshold and your points convert to rewards automatically.

3. Share Rewards

Rewards are split evenly among all students. The more your class recycles, the more everyone earns!

Why We Exist

Our Mission & Vision

At EcoLink, we recognize that the future belongs to those who care for it today. This initiative is more than just a recycling program — it is a leadership platform designed to foster a deep sense of environmental responsibility.

Our primary goal is to transform "waste" into opportunity. By gamifying the process of sustainability across our entire school network, we encourage students to develop lifelong habits of conservation as a team.

Core Values

  • Integrity

    Submitting your class impact honestly for teacher review.

  • Teamwork

    Classrooms share accounts and pool their impact for maximum reward.

  • Global Impact

    Every item saved contributes to a network-wide environmental movement.
